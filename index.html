About Us

Solitaire Confinement is a dynamic and versatile two-piece DIY music project that defies the expectation of sticking to one genre. While we are currently honing in on our industrial sound, we have explored and will continue to explore new styles.

Self produced and experimental in our approach, we create the music we want to. We hope to give catharsis in the music we make. That is our mission as musicians.
